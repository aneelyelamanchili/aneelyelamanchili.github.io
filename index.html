I am Aneel Yelamanchili, an up-and-coming software developer, student, and photographer.

About me

I am an 18 year old software developer from Pleasanton, California. I fell in love with technology at a young age, learning my first language, Lua, in eighth grade. From there I went on to learn other languages such as Java, Python, and C either in the classroom or on my own free time.

During the summer of 2014 I attended Cornell University's Summer College where I took two courses, ENGRG1060 (Exploration in Engineering) and CS1100 (Introduction to Computing Using Python). I gained a good sense of how a product's development process functions, and learned a new programming language that I continue to use daily. I recently graduated Foothill High School and am currently studying computer science at University of Southern California in Los Angeles.

I had the opportunity to work as an intern in Symantec Corporation's Data Loss Prevention group in San Francisco, California. I learned many useful skills in the field of software development during my six month tenure.
